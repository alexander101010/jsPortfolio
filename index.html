About Me

Born and raised in scenic upstate New York, I moved to New York City after studying math and philosophy in Boston. Most of my years in Brooklyn were spent working in the specialty coffee industry, where, in addition to perfecting my espresso skills, I excelled at intuiting customers’ needs and delivering exceptional service under pressure.

Since moving to the Netherlands at the end of 2019, I have refocused my aptitude for and love of math into a new career path. In September 2020, I completed an intensive three-month bootcamp, where I focused on Ruby on Rails. Since then, I have been taking a deeper dive into vanilla JavaScript and subsequently ReactJS, and I am excited to use my range of skills on projects that I am passionate about.
